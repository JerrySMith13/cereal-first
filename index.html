Black Country, New Road

Black Country, New Road are a British post-punk band formed in Cambridge in 2018. They are known for their intense live performances and their eclectic sound, which draws on a wide range of influences, including post-punk, jazz, and spoken word poetry. The band released their debut album, For the first time, in February 2021 to critical acclaim. The album was named one of the best albums of the year by several publications, including NME, The Guardian, and The Quietus.

The band suffered the departure of their lead vocalist and songwriter, Isaac Wood, after the release of their 2023 album Ants From Up There. However, they're coming out swinging with a new album featuring vocals by bassist Tyler Hyde, instrumentalist Georgia Ellery, and pianist May Kershaw.
